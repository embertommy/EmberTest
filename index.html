Our pick of what info you need to know about us and why we are the choice for you.

What is EmberNodes?

EmberNodes is a high spec free host and a very cheap premium host with the utmost priority for the quality of clients servers. We have nodes available in several places of the world, such as Singapore, United Kingdom and France; with many more locations hopefully to come soon. Our premium plans are highly reviewed positively with their extremely low prices and history for 99.9% uptime.

Whats services do we host?

EmberNodes Hosting has potential for hosting most things you can think of with us here at the developing team adding newly requested features and games almost weekly from our suggestions tunnel. Our most popular software/games hosted are Minecraft and discord bot hosting such as node.js and python, this is due to them being in our highly anticipated free plans. In our premium plans however, clients can host a lot more games such as Ark, Rust and more!

What will the price range of our premium servers be?

Unlike most hosts, we plan to have amazingly cheaper plans that have the utmost quality to support a massive amount of players. We estimate our plans will be around 0.75$-1$ per GB, this will be the same for no matter what game you are choosing to host on our services.

When do we plan to release?

We plan to release within the next 2 - 3 weeks, this may seem like a while but we are preparing some machines to handle high amounts of free servers and keep them quality and online 24/7. If you have any questions about this, join the discord.
